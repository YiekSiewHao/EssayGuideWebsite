About EssayGuide

EssayGuide is the ultimate study companion crafted for Malaysian Secondary students aiming to write better Bahasa Malaysia (BM), English (BI), and Bahasa Cina (BC) essays and enrich their vocabulary by providing a comprehensive collection of top-scoring essays, a vocabulary booster, a glossary list, and a word-of-the-day feature.
